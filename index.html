Discover Xcel Hub Training Services

Based in Cubao, Quezon City, Philippines, we are a dedicated tutorial center committed to building digital literacy and job-ready computer skills. Since 2018, we have empowered beginners, freelancers, jobseekers, and professionals through hands-on training in Excel, Google Sheets, Data Management, and Data Analysis.

With small class sizes of up to six students, we ensure focused, personalized learning tailored to your goals. We offer flexible schedules, online and face-to-face sessions, and official certifications as an Authorized Certiport Testing Center - helping you succeed in today's digital workplace.
